Procurement Strategy
Best-in-Class Procurement & ESG

New Balance balances long-term supplier partnerships, responsible purchasing practices, and ambitious environmental goals aligned to its global ESG framework.

ü§ù
Strategic Supplier Partnerships
Builds long-term contracts with textile & footwear suppliers in Vietnam, Taiwan & China. Ensures stable quality, predictable costs, and shared innovation on performance materials. KPI: On-time delivery rate.
üåø
Environmental ESG
Uses recycled polyester, sustainable leather, and eco-cotton. Targets 100% preferred leather (LWG-certified). Reduces carbon emissions through energy efficiency programs in factories. KPI: Material defect rate.
üë•
Social Responsibility
Ensures fair wages and safe working conditions across the supply chain. Conducts regular supplier audits and supports capacity building programs. KPI: Supplier quality index.
üè≠
Vendor-Managed Inventory
Key materials managed through VMI to reduce ordering lead times. Consolidation at origin ports to optimize container utilization and reduce per-unit logistics cost.
üåê
Ocean-First Shipping
Sea freight is the primary inbound mode at 70% of volume. Air freight reserved for high-value product launches and replenishment. Proximity sourcing for US & UK "Made" programs reduces lead times.
üèóÔ∏è
Regional DC Network
Regional distribution centers enable cross-docking and last-mile optimization. Third-party logistics (3PL) partnerships provide scalable capacity for peak seasons and e-commerce volumes.
